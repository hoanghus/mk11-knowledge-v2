# Kollector

The Kollector is part of the Naknada. A race of six-armed humanoids under Shao Khan's ruling. He is driven by the desire to amass wealth and power. Believing he can best do so under Shao Khan's command. Doing his every wish and primarily collecting tribute on his behalf.

## Key Notes

First Appearance: Mortal Kombat 11, 2019
Allies

Shao Khan, master
