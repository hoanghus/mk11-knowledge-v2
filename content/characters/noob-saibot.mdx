# Noob Saibot

The most powerful ninja in the Lin Kuei Clan, Bi-Han was known as Sub-zero. After leading a life of assassinations, violence, and senseless killings, his soul became corrupted. After being murdered by Scorpion as payback he was sent to the Netherrealm wherein Quan Chi would resurrect him and strip him of his humanity.

First Appearance: Mortal Kombat, 1992 (as sub-zero), Mortal Kombat II, 1993
