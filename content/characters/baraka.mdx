# Baraka

Part of the Tarkatan, one of the races in the outworld. Baraka is a fierce warrior under the command of Shao Khan. he quickly rose through the ranks of his master's army and became a general and second in command, below Mileena.

## Key Notes

First appearance: Mortal Kombat II, 1993
Allies

Shao Khan, master
Mileena, commander
