# Jacqui Briggs

Jacqui is the daughter of the veteran fighter of the Outer World Investigation Agency, Jax Briggs. After Jax was resurrected and taken out of the hands of Quan Chi settled down and built a family. Jacqui would become a professional kickboxer and later competed in the Junior Olympics. She would then follow her father's steps and become part of the OIA to protect Earthlearm.

## Key Notes

First appearance: Mortal Kombat X, 2015
Allies

Jax Briggs, Father
Takeda Takahashi, romantic interest
