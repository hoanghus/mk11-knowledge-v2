# Cassie Cage

Cassie Cage is the daughter of Sonya Blade and, as you probably guessed, Johnny Cage. She decides to follow her mother's path and enroll in the special forces. On Raiden's instructions, she gathers a new team of fighters to protect Earth from Shinnok, because old heroes are aging. With new fighters, she participates in special operations both in the Earth Kingdom and outside. 
First, she saves her father Johnny Cage and defeats Shinnok. She then participates in the attack on Kronika's stronghold, who was the guardian of time and rescues Liu Kang. Through victory and gaining the special strength of her father, she manages to bring her mother back to life. After that, she resigns from the post of SWAT commander, refusing to become the new keeper of time.
