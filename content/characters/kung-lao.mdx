# Kung Lao

A Shaolin monk seeking to fill the shoes of a great ancestor, Kung Lao trained under Bo' Rai Cho as part of the Earthrealm warriors. Often Kung Lao lacks the discipline and concentration of his peers, and such comes across as an immature and reckless Shaolin. Many of these acts share a deeper root. One of insecurities and pressure to be as great as his ancestors.

## Key Notes

First Appearance: Mortal Kombat II, 1993
Friends:

Liu Kang
Bo' Rai Cho
Great Kung Lao, ancestor
