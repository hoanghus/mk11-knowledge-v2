# Sheeva

Sheeva is part of the ancient race of the Shokan. Four armed half-humans half-dragons from the Outworld. As with all other members of her species. She is tall, muscular, and with three digits in each hand and two big toes in each foot.
Sheeva was one of the personal bodyguards to Queen Sindel amid the Earthrealm invasion. Later she became the queen of the Shokan after Goro's father's death.
Sheeva's principal concerns surround the Shokan race. She has multiple times betrayed or conspired against enemies and allies alike to ensure the safety and prosperity of her species.

## Key Notes

First Appearance: Mortal Kombat 3, 1995, as a playable character
Rivals:
