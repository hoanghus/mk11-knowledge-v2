# Cetrion

Cetrion is an Elder Goddess, sister to Shinnok, and Daugther of Kronika. She was one of the deities responsible for Shinnok's banishing to the Netherrealm. Her core beliefs center on keeping balance in the world. But beyond that, she will always be loyal to ger mother Kronika. She went as far as to betray other Elder Gods to help further Kronikas plan for a New Era. A pursuit that would eventually weaken her.
