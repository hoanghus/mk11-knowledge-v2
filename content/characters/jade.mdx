# Jade

Born over ten thousand years ago, Jade has been a life-long friend of Kitana and served alongside her and Mileena under Shao Khan's regime. Eventually when Kitana turned against Shao Khan Jade was tasked with killing her. An order she would disobey and instead join her. Jade's loyalty eventually earned her the title of General for the Edenian army.

## Key Notes

First Appearance: Mortal Kombat II, 1993, as a palette swap; Ultimate Mortal Kombat, 2007
Friends List:

Jobashel (father; deceased)
Kitana (life-long friend)
