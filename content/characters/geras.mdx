# Geras

Built by Kronika herself, Geras is a loyal servant of the Keeper of time. Like Kronika, he is capable of manipulating the sands of time. He is immortal and as powerful as a god. Capable of not just learning from his deaths, but improving himself with each iteration of his existence. Furthermore, he can heal any wound in potentially seconds.

## Key Notes

First Appearance: Mortal Kombat 11, April 2019
Rivals

Kung Lao

Allies

Kronika, creator
