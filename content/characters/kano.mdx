# Kano

Kano is a greedy Australian mercenary for the Black Dragon clan. He has gone to great lengths to secure himself a good living. Betraying everyone who he must to do so. Even Earthrealm itself. Once part of Shao Kahn's inner circle, he eventually commanded one of the armies in the Outworld.

## Key Notes

First Appearance: Mortal Kombat I, 1992

Rivals

Sonya Blade

Allies

Shao Kahn
