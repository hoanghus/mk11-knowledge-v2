# Shang Tsung

The host of the Mortal Kombat Tournaments, Shang Tsung is arguably the most powerful sorcerer in all the realms. He can shapeshift into a multitude of forms and even individuals. And with the blessings of Shao Khan and his ability to traverse all worlds undetected. He is a force to be reckoned with, one that even Kronika recognized and used for her plans.

## Key Notes

First Appearance: Mortal Kombat, 1992 (non-playable Boss). Mortal Kombat II, 1993
Rivals

Liu Kang
Kung Lao
Kenshi
