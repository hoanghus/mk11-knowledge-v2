# Jax

Major Jackson "Jax" Briggs is a member of the Outer World Investigation Agency (OIA) from the United States of America. He has undergone various Cybernetic enhancements. After years of service, and after being killed and brought back to life, he retired and formed a family.
Alas, his peaceful and quiet life would not last. His daughter, Jacqui, would follow his path and join the OIA. He would also lose her wife. After many pains and traumas, Kronika tempts him with the possibility of resetting the timeline and giving him another chance to steer his daughter away from the military and potentially save his wife.
