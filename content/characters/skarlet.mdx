# Skarlet

One of the many adopted daughters of Shao Khan. He found Skarlet starving in the streets of the Outworld. Took her under his tutelage and eventually experimented with her using his sorcery. These experiments would empower Skarlet with blood Magik, skills that Shao Khan later helps her develop further.

## Key Notes

First appearance: Mortal Kombat, 2011 (as part of the Komplete Edition)
Allies

Shao Khan, Adoptive Father
