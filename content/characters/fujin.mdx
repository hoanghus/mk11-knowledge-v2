# Fujin

Lord Fujin, Raiden's younger brother, left the Sky Temple to live among humans. In his time among mortals, Fujin became more sympathetic to humans. As he was empathetic of Jax's pain over his failures. Raiden feared that Fujin would grow weaker as a result of these newfound sentiments for humans. Even coming to blows with him in what Fujin said was the only time they fought.

## Key Notes

First Appearance: Mortal Kombat Mythologies: Sub-Zero, 1997, as an unnamed boss. Mortal Kombat 4, 1997, as a playable character.
