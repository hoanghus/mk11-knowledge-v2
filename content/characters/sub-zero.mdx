# Sub-Zero

In the Mortal Kombat universe, there were originally two brothers (the younger Kuai Liang and the elder Bi-Han) Sub-Zero. The elder participated in MK I. He received orders from the head of the assassin clan, The Lin Kuei, to assassinate the Mortal Kombat “tournament manager” Shang Tsung. The elder brother did not succeed in doing this, he was killed by Scorpion, who, in turn, wanted to achieve revenge. After death, elder Sub-Zero becomes evil (Noob Saibot).
In the next part - Mortal Kombat II, the younger Sub-Zero enters the battle.
In the direct sequel to Mortal Kombat II, Bi-Han is replaced by his brother Kuai Liang. He is sent to the tournament after the death of his elder brother. The assignment is all the same - to kill Shang Tsung.
He was then forced to flee and leave his clan The Lin Kuei due to the fact that the leaders decided to turn all the Assassins into cyborgs. 3 cyborgs were sent in pursuit of him, but they failed to kill the younger Sub-Zero. Earlier, he received a vision from Raiden and agreed to join the defense of the Earth.
